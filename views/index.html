Full Package

This is our most comprehensive service that covers all aspects of the planning process from start to finish and beyond. This is for the client who would like the expertise and full support of a professional Planner to ensure their special event far exceeds expectations without the stress and time constraints of organising it themselves.  This is for the  client who wants the best of the best and some  examples of what we assist with include the following –
* Venue search
* Financial planning
* Stage decoration
* Personalised timeline
* Styling and creation
* Floristry
* Lighting, sound, production, bespoke set design & prop hire
* Entertainment sourcing and management
* Photography and videography
* Catering
* Hair and make-up styling
* Attire & Accessories
* Accommodation
* Transport
* Gift registry
* Etiquette and tradition
* Finishing touches
* Full event management
* Pre and post event celebrations
As a guide you can expect to pay between 10 – 15% of the overall budget with a minimum fee applying, however each event is quoted on an individual basis following a consultation and we’re unique in offering a tiered pricing structure .  In all cases, for exceptionally large budgets we are happy to discuss a fixed fee. Partial Package

This service is for the client who wishes to plan the majority of the event themselves but requires professional support on a few key areas, alternatively they may have underestimated the work involved so need a planner to step in and manage the process from that point onwards. The partial planning option is tailored to your personal requirements so you can select a few elements of the full planning service. The only aspect we can’t assist with as part of the partial option is the ‘on the day management’ service. This is for several reasons, but we’ve found in the past that it works much better when we’ve been involved throughout the whole process so we can manage the event much more effectively. Some examples of what we can assist with include the following –
* Venue search
* Styling and creation
* Floristry
* Lighting, sound, production and set design
* Entertainment sourcing and management
* Catering
* Hair and make-up styling
* Attire & accessories
* Accommodation
* Transport
Price is upon quotation depending on the level of assistance required. Please do get in touch to discuss your ideas so far and how we could help by emailing vikas.weddingplanners@gmail.com
